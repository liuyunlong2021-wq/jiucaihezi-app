## 08 | The Second Brain: Where the Idea Came From

The Second Brain concept is inspired by public discussions and systems proposed by **Andrej Karpathy**, an OpenAI co-founder.

The core idea:

> **Let the model look up your memory the way it looks up Wikipedia.**

The idea quickly gained attention:

- Rapid GitHub growth
- A surge of stars
- Broad discussion across the AI community

**Memory should not depend on context alone. It should be searchable, updateable, and reusable.**

---

## 09 | The Second Brain: Five Layers of Skills

We combined Karpathy's idea with the Obsidian knowledge-base approach to build an:

**AI-Wikipedia-style project memory.**

The backend is built on five integrated Skills:

1. **Everything to Wiki** — turn any content into structured knowledge
2. **Raw to Wiki** — convert source facts into reusable knowledge
3. **Query** — retrieve memory like an encyclopedia
4. **Consistency Check** — find continuity errors, missed changes, broken links, and conflicts
5. **Correction** — fix problems at the source of truth

The result:

> **Even in a one-million-word project,**  
> **the AI can still tell you what weapon a minor character was carrying in Chapter 100.**

**Not clumsy. Not forgetful. The details stay where they belong.**

## 10 | The Creative Production Pipeline: Four Steps to a Finished Film

AI filmmaking should not require jumping across a dozen disconnected apps.

Jiucaihezi Studio turns the process into four steps:

1. **Creative Mode + Skills** → Generate production prompts
2. **Creation Panel** → Generate images, video, and audio
3. **Canvas** → Refine the generated material
4. **Creative Mode + Tools** → Assemble the finished film

**Prompt. Generate. Refine. Finish. One connected workflow.**

---

## 11 | Step One: Creative Mode + Skills

Creative Mode and Skills handle the text-production side of the workflow.

Built-in production Skills help break a film into precise, usable prompts:

- Lock the visual style
- Create character prompts
- Create scene prompts
- Create prop prompts
- Create storyboard and shot prompts

This is not generic chat that writes a couple of lines.  
It follows a production workflow and builds the prompts needed for the next stage.

**Define how the film should be made before generating the media.**

---

## 12 | Step Two: The Creation Panel

Once the prompts are ready, move directly into the Creation Panel.

In the same system, you can:

- Select models from China or around the world
- Generate images
- Generate video
- Generate audio

No copying prompts into another app.  
No switching accounts, interfaces, or histories.

**Think on one side. Generate on the other.**

---

## 13 | Step Three: Canvas Refinement

Generated material is not automatically production-ready.

The Canvas lets you:

- Edit images with AI
- Adjust, repair, extend, and align details
- Keep the usable material and correct what is wrong

Your media does not disappear after download.  
It stays inside the project for continued use.

**Generation is the starting point. Refinement makes it production-ready.**

---

## 14 | Step Four: Creative Mode + Tools

After refinement, bring the images, video, and audio back into Creative Mode.

Use the toolchain to:

- Assemble media with tools such as FFmpeg
- Combine assets into a finished film
- Complete the final production steps inside the same project

This is not just a media generator.  
It is a creative system that takes the work all the way to the finish line.

**From prompt to media, from refinement to final cut.**  
**Four steps. One closed loop.**
